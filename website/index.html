Powered by Vector Tiles

Studio provides a new level of design control and interaction. By packing layers, geometries, and feature attributes into lower zoom levels, higher zooms render at a fraction of the storage cost of raster tiles. Paired with Mapnik and a CartoCSS stylesheet, vector tiles can be rendered as images, UTFGrids, geojson, and more.

Access to global data

Studio gives you instant streaming access to Mapbox's massive global datasets: Streets, Terrain, and Satellite. We manage all the data on our platform, providing live updates every minute to all our global data sets, and serve maps fast wherever you are in the world.

Source: Add custom data

Convert traditional geodata sources (Shapefile, GeoJSON, etc.) into vector tiles, upload and reference with Mapbox access token, then style layer-by-layer... all inside Studio.

Composite multiple datasets

Combine your point, polygon, and line datasets into individual layers in one vector tile dataset for styling.

Control your data

Keep your vector tiles small and performant by defining minzoom, maxzoom, and buffer size.

Inspect your data

Peek into your data before rendering and even style locally as you edit down your datasets.

Create custom styles for Mapbox global data and your own custom data in Studio. From there you can customize everything to match your brand: typography, colors, pins, patterns, icons, and more.
