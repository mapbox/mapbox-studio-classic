Powered by Vector Tiles

Studio provides a new level of design control and interaction. By packing layers, geometries, and feature attributes into lower zoom levels, higher zooms render at a fraction of the storage cost of raster tiles. Paired with Mapnik and CartoCSS, vector tiles can be rendered as images, UTFGrids, GeoJSON, and more.

Access to global data

Studio gives you instant streaming access to our massive global datasets: Streets, Terrain, and Satellite. We manage all the data on our platform, providing live updates every minute, and serve maps fast wherever you are in the world.
